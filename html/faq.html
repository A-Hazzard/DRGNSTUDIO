FAQs

YOUR FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ANSWERED WITH EASE!

From when to come to how to get here, and how to have the safest splash of a day, Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) are answered here. We'll worry about the details, so grab a life jacket and all your favorite people and get ready to have some family fun!>

Where is DRGN STUDIO Water Park located?

We are located in Chaguaramas just after the Boardwalk along the coastline of the western peninsula in Trinidad.

Where is the Entrance and Parking for the Park

Parking and Entrance to the Park is at the eastern end of the facility, on the Water Park side of the park.

(Parking lot is right after Pier 1)

Do I have to pay for each ride after paying the entrance fee?

No, the entrance fee includes access to all rides and attractions in the Water and Amusement Parks, unlimited rides.

Note: Paintball and Arcade Games carry separate fees.

Why are admission charges based on height?

Every ride and attraction has been designed by its manufacturer with specific safety guidelines for their use based on height. Guests with heights over 48” can use all the rides and attractions in the park, while guests with heights of 47” and under can use less attractions, hence the lower price for guests in this price bracket.

Does the park have a dress code?

We ask guests to respect that our park is a family-friendly facility and as a result, the swimwear worn at Five Islands Water and Amusement Park must be appropriate and not offensive to other guests in the park. Recommended Swimming Attire: We strongly recommend rash guards, swim shorts, whole piece or one-piece swimsuits be worn by ladies, due to the force of the water on some attractions. Men can wear appropriate swim shorts, rash guards and bare back is permitted. Children not yet toilet trained are required to wear approved swim diapers. (Available for purchase at the park) Water or swim shoes are permitted in the pools, but not on the slides. Guests are required to wear proper dry clothing and shoes to use the dry attractions in the Amusement Park. (Swimwear, bareback and bare feet are not permitted on the Amusement Park attractions) Swimwear Not Permitted: Swimwear with buckles, zippers or exposed metal accessories are not permitted on slides.
