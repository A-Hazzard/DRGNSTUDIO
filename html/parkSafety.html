PleaseWhere is DRGN STUDIO Water Park located?

We are located in Chaguaramas just after the Boardwalk along the coastline of the western peninsula in Trinidad.

Where is the Entrance and Parking for the Park

Parking and Entrance to the Park is at the eastern end of the facility, on the Water Park side of the park.
(Parking lot is right after Pier 1)

What are the prices of the park?


2 FOR 1 SPECIAL - $250 for 2 persons. This ends on 30th March 2022

SPECIAL ADMISSION PRICES FOR EASTER:

Guests 59″ & over $200

Guests 36″ - 58″ $100

Guests under 36″ Free

This includes all attractions in the Water Park and the Amusement Park. Access is unlimited, all day long.

Paintball and Arcade Games carry separate fees

Paintball is available but at a separate Group price. Bookings are required. Please call 612-7275 (PARK) or email info@5islandspark.com to make bookings.

Do I have to pay for each ride after paying the entrance fee?


No, the entrance fee includes access to all rides and attractions in the Water and Amusement Parks, unlimited rides.

Note: Paintball and Arcade Games carry separate fees.
