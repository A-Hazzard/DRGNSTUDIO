Jobs in Los Angeles, CA

At Raging Waters Los Angeles we not only offer employment but the opportunity to meet new friends and have fun in the process! If you are looking for a summer job that does more than pay you for your time, Raging Waters has the job for you! Many of our Team Members have never worked before. They find that the friendly and fun atmosphere here is a great place to get experience.

Become a Team Member

Gain valuable experience Make friends with talented people of all ages Receive a paycheck and enjoy outstanding perks

Perks of the Job

Flexible schedules Free admission to parks Food and retail discounts Recognition program Advancement opportunities

How to Apply

If you are ready to become a part of our exciting team, click the link below to view current openings at the Palace Entertainment / Raging Waters Los Angeles (San Dimas) Career Center.

1) Once on the Careers Page, you will need to create a "Candidate Profile" by Registering on our site. 2) Once registered, you may click on any open position and complete and submit an online application. 3) After that, simply check your email regularly for notices on the status of your application.
